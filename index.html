📚 Water Education

Water Quality Guide 💧

Understanding your water quality is the first step to a healthier life. Know the TDS levels and what they mean for you.

⚠️
0 – 50 ppm
Too Low
Lacks essential minerals. Can leach minerals from your body. Not ideal for regular consumption.
✅
50 – 150 ppm
Excellent
Perfect TDS range. Ideal mineral balance. Best for health and taste. WHO recommended range.
👍
150 – 300 ppm
Good
Acceptable quality. RO purifier can further improve taste and remove hidden contaminants.
⚡
300 – 500 ppm
Moderate
Above ideal. Slight taste and health risk. RO purification is strongly recommended.
🚫
500 – 900 ppm
High — Purify
Unsafe for regular consumption. Health risks possible. Immediate RO installation needed.
🆘
900+ ppm
Dangerous!
Extremely high TDS. Health hazard. Contact Waterman Purifiers immediately for urgent help!
